# Contribution Schedule of the Agentic Organisations Collective

> **Non-binding English translation.** This is a courtesy translation of the
> German contribution schedule
> ([`contribution-rules.md`](contribution-rules.md)) for members and interested
> persons. Only the German version (*Beitragsordnung*) is legally binding. In the
> event of any discrepancy between the two versions, the German text prevails.
> References to German statutes (e.g. `Abgabenordnung`) are retained because they
> designate the applicable German law.

This contribution schedule supplements the statutes of the Agentic Organisations
Collective. It regulates the type, amount and due date of the membership
contributions on the basis of § 6 of the statutes. The general meeting resolves
on it; it is not part of the statutes and may be amended by simple majority.

## § 1 Principle

(1) The association levies annual contributions from its members and supporting
members to cover the ongoing costs and to promote the purpose of the association.

(2) Full membership exists as individual membership (natural persons) or as
organisational membership (legal persons and partnerships with legal capacity).
Organisational members designate a natural person who exercises their
participation and their voting right.

(3) Decisive for the contribution are the type of membership as well as, in the case of companies, the number of employees.

## § 2 Annual Contributions of Full Members

(1) The annual contribution amounts to:

| Type of membership | Annual contribution |
| --- | --- |
| Individual members (natural persons) | 60 euros |
| Small companies (up to 50 employees) | 120 euros |
| Large companies (more than 50 employees) | 360 euros |
| Research institutions, public corporations and non-profit organisations | 120 euros |

(2) Classification is based on the information provided in the membership
application. Employees are deemed to be the employees subject to compulsory
social insurance, calculated in full-time equivalents. Research institutions,
public corporations and non-profit organisations are assigned to the reduced tier
regardless of their size; non-profit organisations are deemed to be corporations
with recognised tax privilege under §§ 51 et seq. of the Fiscal Code
(*Abgabenordnung*), in particular non-profit limited liability companies (gGmbH),
non-profit associations and foundations. If a member is to be classified both as
a company and as a non-profit organisation, the reduced tier always applies. If
the relevant number of employees of a company changes, the classification is to
be adjusted as of the following financial year.

## § 3 Supporting Members

(1) Supporting members support the association exclusively financially or in
spirit, without assuming association responsibility.

(2) Supporting members freely choose the amount of their annual contribution; the
minimum contribution is 60 euros. Higher contributions are welcome.

## § 4 Contribution Exemption for Office Holders

(1) Individual members who hold an office on the board or on the advisory board
(*Kuratorium*) are exempt from the contribution for the duration of their term of
office. If a person holds the office as a representative of an organisational
member, the contribution of the organisational member remains in place.

(2) The exemption compensates for the voluntary effort, ends with the end of the
term of office and does not establish any monetary benefit beyond that.

## § 5 Beginning of Membership and Due Date

(1) Membership begins upon admission by the board. The first annual contribution
becomes due upon admission.

(2) The annual contribution is due at the beginning of each financial year. In
the case of joining during the current financial year, the full annual
contribution for the current year is to be paid.

(3) The contribution is generally paid by bank transfer or by a direct debit
mandate granted by the member.

## § 6 Contribution Adjustment and Termination

(1) Membership continues until it is terminated in accordance with § 5 of the
statutes. The annual contribution is to be paid anew for each financial year.

(2) Contributions already paid are not refunded upon termination of membership.
The association's claim to outstanding contributions remains unaffected.

(3) The board decides at its dutiful discretion on reductions or deferrals in
justified individual cases, for instance for low-income persons.

## § 7 Entry into Force

and enters into force with this resolution.
